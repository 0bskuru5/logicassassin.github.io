Entoto National Park

Description

Entoto Park, like Unity Park, is a project initiated by the Prime Minister of Ethiopia, Abiy Ahmed. The park was opened to visitors on September 30th, 2020, and has quickly become a popular weekend retreat and day out for locals and tourists alike. Entoto Park is located on a mountain which is often referred to as 'the lung of Addis Ababa'. The mountain is covered with lush eucalyptus forest, which was previously of little use. However, the park has transformed this previously overlooked forest into a mesmerizing park with a bounty of services and activities. The park is a multi-purpose destination that caters to the diverse interests of its visitors. It contains both indoor and outdoor facilities built mostly with local materials. These facilities include cafes, restaurants, horseback riding, spas, an artificial lake, go-karts, biking, a library, a cinema, as well as sports centers. Visitors can enjoy a taste of authentic Ethiopian coffee, served by major brands in the country such as Kaldis' and Tomoca. Pioneering Ethiopian resort, Kuriftu, also has a charming and capacious restaurant within the park. For art lovers, the park has art galleries showcasing the work of local artists. Additionally, there are children's play areas for families to enjoy

Overall, Entoto Park offers visitors a wide range of activities and amenities to enjoy. It is a great place to experience the natural beauty of Ethiopia while also indulging in a variety of recreational activities.

What to Expect

Entoto Park offers a variety of activities and amenities for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the things you can expect to find in the park:

  1. Lush Eucalyptus Forest: Entoto Park is located on a mountain covered with lush eucalyptus forest. Visitors can enjoy the natural beauty of the forest while exploring the park.
  2. Cafes and Restaurants: The park offers various cafes and restaurants where visitors can taste authentic Ethiopian coffee and cuisine. Popular Ethiopian brands such as Kaldis' and Tomoca are served, and Kuriftu, a pioneering Ethiopian resort, has a restaurant within the park.
  3. Recreational Activities: Entoto Park offers several recreational activities such as horseback riding, go-karts, biking, and an artificial lake where visitors can go boating. The park also has sports centers where visitors can engage in various sports activities.
  4. Spa and Wellness: Visitors can relax and unwind in the park's spa and wellness facilities.
  5. Art Galleries: The park has art galleries showcasing the work of local artists.
  6. Children's Play Areas: The park has designated children's play areas, making it an excellent destination for families with kids.
  7. Library and Cinema: Entoto Park has a library and a cinema where visitors can enjoy books and movies.

Overall, Entoto Park offers a variety of activities and amenities that cater to the diverse interests of its visitors. It is an excellent destination to experience the natural beauty of Ethiopia while indulging in recreational activities and exploring Ethiopian culture.
