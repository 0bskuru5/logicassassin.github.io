Jazz Night

Description

Ethiopia is home to a unique fusion of jazz and traditional Ethiopian music called "Ethio Jazz," which dates back to the 1950s. This innovative genre combines the eerie rhythms of ancient Ethiopian cultural music with the soulful undertones of jazz and the funky bounce of Afro-beat. Ethio-jazz features the typical haunting and ancient-sounding tones of traditional Ethiopian music, blended with the sensual undertones of soulful jazz. Unfortunately, the communist regime temporarily suspended this genre (along with other types of live music), but it has recently made a comeback and can be enjoyed at various venues across Addis Ababa. If you are a fan of jazz music, Mama's Kitchen around Wollo Sefer is a standout place in the city, and there are also scheduled performances at Ghion Hotel and Jazz Amba Taitu Hotel.
